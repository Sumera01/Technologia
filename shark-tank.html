SHARK TANK 2025

Present your innovative business ideas to our panel in a real-time startup pitching event inspired by the famous "Shark Tank" format. Showcase creativity, market understanding, and entrepreneurial skills to impress the judges.

Venue

Rules & Regulations

Eligibility

  • Open to all college students (undergraduate, postgraduate & Diploma).
  • Participate in teams (3 to 5 members).
  • Any ethical business idea is allowed.

Event Day Rules

  • Arrive 30 minutes early for check-in and setup.
  • Late arrivals will be disqualified.
  • Bring all necessary presentation materials (ppt/poster/product).
  • Maintain professionalism throughout.

Time Limits

  • 5-minute pitch.
  • 5-minute Q&A by judges.
  • Exceeding time limits may lead to penalties or disqualification.
  • Judges' decisions will be final.
