Rules & Regulations

Participation & Format

  • Strictly team of 2 to 4 members.
  • One team argues “For the motion” (Proposition), the other “Against” (Opposition).
  • Each team gets 11 to 15 minutes for arguments and rebuttals.

Content & Conduct

  • Arguments must be relevant, factual, and logical.
  • Use of statistics, quotes, examples, and real-world references is encouraged.
  • No personal attacks, hate speech, or inappropriate language (violators will be disqualified).
  • Rebuttals must be respectful, fact-based, and to the point.

Materials & Judging

  • Electronic devices are strictly prohibited.
  • Only cue cards, handwritten notes, or printed material are allowed.
  • Judging based on Content, Delivery, Rebuttal, Teamwork, and Time Management.
  • Judges' decisions are final.
