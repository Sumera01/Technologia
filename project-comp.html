MINI PROJECT COMPETITION 2025

Showcase your creativity, innovation, and technical skills through a mini project based on real-world problems. Impress the evaluators with your model, execution, and explanation.

Register Now

Venue

Third Floor, Lab 305, D.Y. Patil School of Engineering & Management, Kolhapur

Rules & Regulations

Eligibility

  • Open to all UG, PG, and Diploma students across all branches.
  • Teams of 2-4 participants.
  • Projects must address real-world problems.

Event Structure

  • Each team gets a specific time slot to present their project to the judges.
  • Projects must include a working model or prototype.
  • Presentations should cover the problem, solution, and technical details.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Innovation: Originality and creativity of the project.
  • Functionality: Practicality and effectiveness of the solution.
  • Technical Accuracy: Soundness of technical implementation.
  • Presentation & Teamwork: Clarity of explanation and collaboration.
  • Judges' decisions are final.
