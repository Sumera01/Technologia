Rules & Regulations

Eligibility & Participation

  • Open to all college students (UG, PG, Diploma).
  • Teams of up to 6 participants.
  • Each team receives a virtual budget of ₹120 crore.

Team Composition Rules

  • Total Players: 15 per team.
  • Indian Players: Minimum 10.
  • Overseas Players: Maximum 5.
  • Exceeding the ₹120 crore budget results in disqualification.

Bidding & Evaluation

  • Teams bid for real IPL players based on their base prices and ratings (out of 10, fixed).
  • Evaluation based on total player ratings and team balance/composition.
  • Judges' decisions are final.
