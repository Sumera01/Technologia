Rules & Regulations

Team & Gameplay

  • Only registered squads of exactly 4 players allowed.
  • Custom Room Matches in Classic Mode (TPP/FPP).
  • Maps will be pre-declared.

Devices & IGN

  • Mobile-only (no emulators or external devices).
  • No hacks or third-party apps permitted.
  • Players must use registered in-game names (IGN) only.

Communication & Conduct

  • Voice chat allowed within squad only; global mic usage prohibited.
  • No teaming with other squads.
  • Any misconduct results in instant disqualification.

Punctuality & Judging

  • Join rooms on time as per the official schedule.
  • Rankings based on observer panel and in-game stats.
  • Organizers’ decisions are final.
  • Raise disputes immediately after the match.
