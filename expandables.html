Rules & Regulations

Team & Eligibility

  • Open to all UG, PG, and Diploma students.
  • Teams of exactly 6 participants.

Competition Format

  • Multiple rounds with unique team-based challenges.
  • Challenges involve strategy, physical, and mental coordination.
  • Last team in each round is eliminated.
  • Last remaining team wins.

Task & Judging

  • Tasks may be time-based or rank-based (completion order).
  • Rule violation (e.g., dropping a ball in a balance task) requires restarting the task.
  • Round rules announced at the start of each round.
  • Judges' decisions are final.
